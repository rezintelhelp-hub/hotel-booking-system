Data Deletion

Last updated: February 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

If you have connected your Facebook account to GAS and would like us to delete your data, you can do so in two ways:

Option 1: Remove from GAS Admin

1
Log in to your GAS Admin dashboard at admin.gas.travel
2
Go to Connections in the sidebar
3
Find your Facebook connection and click Disconnect
4
Your Facebook Page Access Token and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 24 hours

Option 2: Remove from Facebook

1
Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy → Settings
2
Click Apps and Websites under Security
3
Find gas.travel and click Remove
4
This will revoke our access and we will delete your stored data within 24 hours

Option 3: Contact Us Directly

If you prefer, you can request data deletion by contacting us directly. We will process your request within 48 hours and confirm deletion by email.

Contact for data deletion requests:

Email: privacy@gas.travel

Please include the email address associated with your GAS account.

What Data We Delete

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently remove: your Facebook Page Access Token, your Facebook Page ID and name, any campaign data associated with your Facebook connection, and any other data obtained through the Facebook integration. This does not affect posts that have already been published to your Facebook Page — those remain on Facebook and can be managed through Facebook directly.
